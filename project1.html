Modern Villa

Residential Project in Mumbai

Project Description

This project was a complete redesign of a suburban villa, transforming it into a luxurious and energy-efficient modern home. We focused on open-plan living, maximizing natural light, and incorporating sustainable materials throughout the design.

Client Brief

The client's main request was to create a spacious, contemporary home with a strong connection to its surrounding landscape.

Key Features

  • Passive solar design
  • Rainwater harvesting system
  • Custom-designed minimalist interiors
  • Integrated smart home technology
