About Ms. Jagruti Bolkowode

Ms. Jagruti Bolkowode is a renowned architect with over 15 years of experience in creating innovative and sustainable designs. A graduate of a prestigious university, her work spans residential, commercial, and urban planning projects. She founded her firm with the belief that design should be a collaborative process, focusing on the client's needs while pushing the boundaries of creativity.
